Welcome to the Library Management System

Our Library Management System helps you efficiently manage authors, books, borrowers, and borrowing transactions all in one place. With a simple and intuitive interface, you can easily track and maintain records with just a few clicks.

Features

  • Manage Authors: Add, edit, view, and delete author information.
  • Manage Books: Keep track of books, including title, author, year, and genre.
  • Manage Borrowers: Maintain borrower details like name, email, and phone.
  • Manage Transactions: Record borrowing transactions and track book returns.

Get Started

Use the navigation menu at the top to access and manage different sections. Enjoy a seamless experience managing your library's resources.
